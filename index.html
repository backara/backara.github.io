What is SOFO?

Product sourcing platform
for online sellers.

After COVID-19, total number of online sellers has grown to 1 million. SOFO is a video based app for B2B online sellers and microfactories.

The small micro-factories can list their products, inventories, catalogues etc in the video form on SOFO app. It's convenient for the online sellers to check products in the form of videos, and choose the best supplier. Both the parties can communicate through inbuilt chat feature available in the SOFO app.

Besides these, SOFO also provides other important tools like data analytics, product catalogue images, SEO product description content etc that are crucial for success in online selling.

Online Sellers

State of Online Sellers

With the increase in online shoppers due to COVID-19 and home quarantine, the number of online sellers has also increased drastically on E-commerce marketplaces.

Now the online sellers are facing stiff competition on E-commerce marketplaces. So if they don't become smart and strategic, then it's very tough to generate profits.

New online sellers find it difficult to choose the best product category that can give them maximum profits with the least risk.

They need product catalogue images, product description content, keywords etc to list the products in a better way. SOFO app will provide these services in Premium package.

They find it challenging to source products in small quantities because manufacturers don't entertain small buyers.

Micro Factories

State of Micro Factories

Micro factories get overshadowed in the presence of large manufacturers who can get better leads from the market. Micro-factories are the best parties for the case of online sellers.

  • Micro factories can fulfill orders even in small quantities, which are best of online sellers.
  • There is no dedicated platform for micro factories that can escape competition from large suppliers. SOFO app is exclusively for micro factories.
  • Micro factories are the backbone of the country's economy. They need advanced software tools for lead generation and catalogue distribution. SOFO app will provide them these tools.
  • Micro factories are willing to provide customisation in small quantity orders. So they can help new sellers to create new brand labels on online marketplaces.

What is SOFO?

SOFO is a B2B video app made for online sellers and microfactories, to help them do business in a much better way. SOFO app provides the data and analytics tools, photography catalogues, etc to increase the sales of online sellers.

What are the team members?

The team members behind the SOFO project are highly experienced in E-commerce industry since 2013. They have built software platforms for E-commerce users, business clients, wholesalers, resellers etc. The combined team has robust experience in software, marketing, customer service, e-commerce, B2B etc.

Who can use the SOFO app?

SOFO app is built for two parties- Online sellers who sell products on e-commerce marketplaces, and Micro-factories who have small manufacturing units.

What is the use of SOFO app?

SOFO app will be used by the online sellers to source products from micr-factories and sell them on online marketplaces. Micro-factories will post videos of their products, workplace etc on SOFO app to generate leads.

In which countries will the SOFO app work?

Starting from India, we'll expand to other countries in Asia, Europe and Americas.

What is SOFO Token Sales?

We are raising funds from the community to work on our projected roadmap, with an accelerated speed. This sale is also to ensure wide distribution of SOFO tokens in the public.

When will the token sale start and end?

The sale is scheduled in 3 rounds. The first phase is Seed round to be held in the last week of March 2021. The second phase is called Private round to be held between April 25 to May 10, 2021. The last round is Public sale scheduled between May 10 to May 25, 2021.

Who can invest in this Token sale?

Anyone investing in our sale should understand the risks of a crypto project. We have set limits for investment in different rounds. The seed round and private round is only for accredited investors. The public sale is open for all, except from the restricted countries where local laws prohibit participating in token sales.

What are the stages of this Token sale?

There are 3 stages in this sale. Seed round, Private round and Public sale.

What is the price of token in sales?

The price of token is different in different rounds of the sales. It is set so as to reward the early investors. The price of token in different rounds are as follows: Seed round- $0.006, Private round- $0.012 and Public sale- $0.015

How can I participate in this token sales?

To participate in the private round, contact our token sales team at info@sofo.tech . To participate in our Public sale, kindly wait for the announcement in our community forums.

What crypto can I use to purchase tokens?

You can purchase our tokens with BNB in the public sale.

What type of token is SOFO?

SOFO is a utility token built on BSC chain for the SOFO ecosystem. It is a BEP20 standard token.

What is the utility of SOFO tokens?

There will be multiple utility of SOFO token in SOFO ecosystem. Initially, SOFO token will be used as a payment mode for using any services in the SOFO app. Once the user base of SOFO app increases, then SOFO token will be used to provide access of Global credit to our community.

How many SOFO tokens are there?

The maximum supply of SOFO is 100,000,000 tokens.

What is the burning mechanism of token?

For each and every usage of services in the SOFO app, SOFO tokens will be charged. Out of which, 20% of the collected tokens will be burned forever. With more users and more usage of SOFO platform, this burnt tokens amount could be a bigger percentage of circulating supply leading towards the scarcity of SOFO tokens.
